    Student FAQ Tutor FAQ

How do I find a tutor?

Complete the enquiry form and we will be in touch with you soon to connect you with your tutor and to arrange the lessons

How does the process work?

Step 1: Complete the enquiry form

Step 2: Someone from SkillUp, (the course administrators) will contact you to tell you more.

Step 3: We'll match you with a tutor and class in your area at a time that suits you.

Step 4: Once each lesson is completed, your tutor will mark the lesson completed and provide feedback and notes, which will be emailed to you

Why 15 weeks?

The course, originally designed by the late Len Keating, is designed to be completed in 15 weeks. Week 16 is set aside for the students and tutor to have a social get-together, ideally at the home or in the community of the tutor. May this be the start of many friendships!

How much does it cost?
Number of students in the group Fee per student
1 R3000
2 R2200
3-5 R1800
6-10 R1600

Each student also receives a CD in week 3, the cost of which is included in the total amount.

Is tutoring safe?

Safety is of highest importance to Imbeko.

  1. Tutors are required to get police clearance from the SAPS, before even having access to the market.
  2. For your or a tutors first lesson, an accredited Imbeko representative can be requested to attend the beginning of the lesson to introduce both parties.
  3. The entire system is built on ratings, a low rated tutor will not receive more bookings.

For more information, please see our safety policy for students and tutors or contact us with further questions

How do I sign up as a tutor?

Head over to the "sign up as a tutor" page and fill out your name, email address and password. These are your login details. From here you will be directed to your own home page where you will be required to complete your profile.

How can I complete my profile?

Completing your profile is easily done once you have created a profile here. There are five main sections you will have to complete before becoming a tutor:

Basic information

Including your address, phone number, and telling us a bit about yourself.

Documentation

Uploading of your ID document, Work Permit (if applicable), CV, Matric-certificate (if you have one) and Safety Clearance.

Language

Specify what language you want to tutor (isiXhosa or isiZulu).

Availability

Complete your calendar showing your availability.

Once you have submitted your application, you will be contacted by an Imbeko staff member and will go through our training process before being listed as one of our tutors.

Why 15 weeks?

The course, originally designed by the late Len Keating, is designed to be completed in 15 weeks. Week 16 is set aside for the students and tutor to have a social get-together, ideally at the home or in the community of the tutor. And may this be the start of many friendships!

How much will I earn?

Students each pay between R1 600 and R3 000 for the course, depending on group size. After our service costs, banking fees, manual and cd costs are deducted, tutors will earn between R150 and R720 per session, depending on the size of the group. The higher you are rated by your students, the more likely you are to get more bookings!

You will be paid out every Wednesday for the previous week's lessons

How do I get safety clearance?

It is part of Imbeko’s policy that you require Police Clearance, which can be done at your local police station for R96 + postage (+- R100). This can take 4-8 weeks.
