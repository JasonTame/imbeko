How it Works

01

Fill in the enquiry form

Specify the language you want to learn, and your area, and also your group size: whether you are wanting individual lessons, or have a group already set up.

02

Discuss & Arrange Lessons

Once you have been matched to a trained tutor, you can arrange a lesson schedule through our online messaging system

03

Pay for Lessons Securely Online

Buying lessons is easy: once your group size is confirmed, pay easily online with EFT, Credit Card, or Snapscan. Payment includes the full 15-week course and an “Everyday Xhosa Phrases” CD, which you will receive at your third lesson.

04

Take the Course and Speak the Language

Lessons can happen wherever you arrange, once a week. You will receive emailed feedback and notes after every lesson, so you can track your progress.

05

End it with a Party

Once you and your group have completed the 15 week course, your Tutor will arrange for a social event in his/her community to end things off. We’d love you to keep having classes every few weeks with your Tutor, just to practice – you can arrange that between your group and the Tutor directly!
